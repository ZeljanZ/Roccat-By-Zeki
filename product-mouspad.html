Sense AIMO

  • Vivid AIMO illumination
  • 2 individually controlled RGB zones
  • Detachable braided cable

$39.99

SENSE THE LIGHT

The Sense joins the AIMO fight to make the lighting eco-system bigger and better than ever before, for an even more immersive experience. Its soft cloth surface is bordered by a vivid RGB light guide with two programmable zones.

VIVID AIMO ILLUMINATION

AIMO is an illumination eco-system that reacts fluently to your usage and syncs with compatible devices to present vivid lighting scenarios. The Sense AIMO showcases the platform in all its glory with a bright RGB light guide.Its powered by a discreet braided cable that can be detached. It also features a low-profile button which allows you to cycle through effects on the fly.

TOP-TIER CLOTH SURFACE

The flexible cloth surface is machine stitched to provide fast speed with decisive control. It is expertly tailored with premium materials to give you precise tracking with a soft touch. Focused attention was given to the border area featuring the light guide.

RUBBERIZED NON-SLIP BACKING

The back of a mousepad is typically it’s most overlooked part – even though it’s amongst its most important. Without a solid foundation your pad ends up slipping all over your desk, which is the last thing you want when you’re in game.
